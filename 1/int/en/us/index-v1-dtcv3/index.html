Does Boltz Projector support Bluetooth audio and external speakers?

Yes, Boltz Projector has Bluetooth capability so you can pair Bluetooth headphones or speakers for audio output without using wires.

What is the built-in storage capacity, and what is it used for?

Boltz Projector has 32 GB internal storage. This is used for storing media files, apps (if smart-OS), or connecting USB drives, depending on how the system is set up.

Can I connect it wirelessly to my phone or laptop?

With Bluetooth plus possibly WiFi or screen casting (if the OS supports it), you should be able to mirror or stream from your devices.

What’s required for setup?

Plug-in power, possibly extending/placing projector at appropriate distance, adjust keystone (if included), focus, etc.

What picture quality does Boltz Projector deliver?

Boltz Projector delivers crisp, high-definition visuals with vibrant colors, ensuring a theater-like experience right in your home.

Can Boltz Projector be used for gaming?

Yes, with its sharp resolution and smooth performance, Boltz Projector delivers an immersive gaming experience on a big screen.
